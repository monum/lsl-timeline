    Scoping the Project

    This week, Local Sense Lab members sat down with the Downtown Business Improvement District (BID), a collaboration of business owners and property managers in downtown Boston. Our goal was to understand what questions the BID wants to investigate about how pedestrians and cars use the Downtown Crossing intersection, and to see if the Lab's researchers and technology could play a supporting role in that investigation.

      We Won!

      The Local Sense Lab was one of five teams--from a pool of almost 100 from all corners of the world --to receive the Global Cities Team Challenge Leadership Award at this week's GCTC conference in Austin. The GCTC conference is organized by the National Institute for Standards and Technology and by US Ignite.
